Personal Interests

I love camping. My favorite place to be in the summer is up in the Uinta National Forest. I also do quite a bit of white water river running. The past two summers I have been to The blackfoot river in Montana, and the section of the green that runs through dinosaur national monument. This summer I'm headed up to run the Rogue river; a once in a lifetime opportunity.

>

I also love to cook. I served an LDS mission in Italy, where I learned a love for simple healthy italian cuisine. Since coming home, I've learned how to prepare dishes from all over the world, but italian remains my favorite. Its my dream to one day own a wood fire pizza oven and make pizza all the time for friends and family.

I've also found a love for coding in recent years. When I initially returned from my mission, I began studying CS because of the abundance of Jobs and the quality of pay. I soon realized that I found a lot of satisfaction in completing projects, and I constantly feel satisfaction at work when I complete a new or difficult component in the software we're writing.
